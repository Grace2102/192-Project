About Us

At Nifty Thrifty we offer quality food from our online food outlet store. Our mission is to provide nutritional and affordable produce to people of low socioeconomic classes as well as to reduce food wastage, which has a direct negative impact on the environment. Food that ends up in landfills negatively impacts biodiversity, builds up methane through the decomposition of food without oxygen which ends up releasing greenhouse gasses into the atmosphere, and wastes millions of gallons of drinkable water through the production of food and livestock. We are beneficial to our customers and the environment because we use produce that is already available to you, myself, and the general public. Nifty Thrifty promotes sustainable consumption through green living while ensuring that the high quality food is available to people and families of low socioeconomic classes.
